# PRD: Astra EA

## 1. Vision
Astra EA is a calm, reliable, and invisible personal operating system that lives inside WhatsApp. It is a high-fidelity bridge between messy communication and structured action.

## 2. The Problem
High-output individuals often lose context between a thought (on the go) and the action (in the tool). Jumping between apps (WhatsApp to Calendar, WhatsApp to Gmail) creates friction that leads to lost opportunities and mental load.

## 3. Target User
- **Profile**: Solo founders, executives, or high-agency individuals.
- **Environment**: Primarily mobile-first communication, frequently on the move.

## 4. Core Features (MVP)
### 4.1 WhatsApp-Native Interface
- Listen to self-chat, voice notes, and forwarded messages.
- Silent operation by default; only speaks when a task is recognized or a decision is needed.

### 4.2 Intent Parsing (Intelligence)
- Convert natural language and voice notes into structured JSON.
- Support for: "Remind me to...", "Schedule a meeting with...", "Draft an email to...".

### 4.3 Google Ecosystem Integration
- **Calendar**: Create events, check availability.
- **Gmail**: Create drafts for follow-ups.

### 4.4 Approval Workflow
- **Crucial**: No external action (sending email, booking calendar) happens without a "Confirm" reaction or keyword in the chat.

## 5. Non-Goals
- Multi-user support.
- General-purpose "chatting" or therapy.
- Complex dashboards or mobile apps.

## 6. Success Metrics
- **Capture-to-Action Time**: < 30 seconds.
- **Parsing Accuracy**: > 95%.
- **Approval Rate**: High user confidence in suggested actions.
